How it works

From requisition to delivered, in days.

i

Describe the need

"I need 500 cotton t-shirts delivered to Dubai by March." That's the input. No forms.

AI shortlists

The Manager benchmarks pricing, sources qualified vendors, and drafts the RFQ. You review.

iii

Quotes come back. The AI counters, follows up, books logistics. You stay in the loop.

iv

Goods land in our warehouse. We QA, store, and ship to your buyer or back to you. Done.
